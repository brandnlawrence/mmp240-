How To Get Away With Murder

2014, TV-14, 6 Seasons

Discover the electrifying world of legal drama and deception with 'How To Get Away With Murder.' Follow the brilliant and enigmatic law professor, Annalise Keating, as she navigates the complexities of the legal system while mentoring a group of ambitious law students. As secrets unravel and tensions rise, watch as they learn the true meaning of justice and the lengths they'll go to protect themselves. Prepare for a rollercoaster of twists, turns, and shocking revelations that will keep you on the edge of your seat. Are you ready to unravel the mystery and learn how to get away with murder?
